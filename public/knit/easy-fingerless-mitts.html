Easy Fingerless Mitts

Status: Finished
Started: October 27, 2025
Finished: November 16, 2025
Yarn: Bernat Super Value - Sedona Sunset Ombre
Skeins: Half a skein
Needles: US 7 (4.5 mm)
Link to Tutorial

This was my first knitting project! From it, I learned how to knit, purl, do a long-tail cast on, a regular bind off, seaming, frogging, and how to put dropped stitches back on the needle.

November 6, 2025
Finished my first glove using English style/throw knitting.

November 12, 2025
Working on my second glove using Continental style. I was looking for ways to speed things up so I moved from throwing to flicking, but that was too hard. Continental ended up being really intuitive for me.
My stitches on the second glove aren't as tight as the first - I don't know if that's a good thing. But being able to knit faster is awesome.

November 16, 2025
Done!! Gave this to a friend!! She loves it :^)
The second glove is slightly shorter than the first but I didn't notice until everything's done and wrapped up - oh well!
