Home

Welcome to my home on the web!

I built this website to document everything I love. Over the past few years, I’ve come to realize how detrimental social media has been to my mental health, and how creating my own website is actually the perfect solution to many of my problems.

I made this site for me, and me only! But I do hope you enjoy your time here. Please feel free to poke around, explore, and sign my guestbook if you’d like ✿

Many pages on this site (like this one!) are lovingly hand-coded, while others (such as my commonplace book) are generated using 11ty. The whole thing is meant to be mobile-friendly, but it’s very much a living, breathing work in progress - so you may run into the occasional broken link or odd layout along the way.
