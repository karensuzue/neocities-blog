About

Pictures of characters and people I resonate with/admire!

Hi, I'm Karen! I am a PhD student and budding researcher with interests spanning computation, complex systems, and evolutionary biology.

That's how I typically introduce myself to people these days. It sounds really cool, but this site isn't about my research. This website is a place to collect photos, notes, projects, and various ramblings and happenings from my personal life. As much as I love my current line of work, this site is my refuge from the "real" world; a way to live out my childhood dream of being a webmaster, and a space to celebrate the parts of myself that don't always get as much attention anymore.

Outside of academia, I am a formally trained artist and a less formally competent ballet school graduate (I attended the Ho Chi Minh City Intermediate Dance School for 6 years, though I wouldn't call myself an actual ballet dancer). I also underwent classical piano training for over a decade, from 2006-2017, but I haven't played seriously in years. More recently, I picked up knitting in October 2025, which has quickly become one of my current hyperfixations.

If you care about personality typologies, I am an INFP-T. For my astrology-inclined friends, my tropical big three are Gemini rising, Scorpio sun, and Scorpio moon. My sidereal big three are Taurus rising, Libra sun, and Scorpio moon. My Vedic nakshatras are Rohini rising, Vishakha sun, and Anuradha moon.

Anyway, thank you so much for being here, and welcome! I hope you enjoy your stay.
