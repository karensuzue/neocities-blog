My first blog post!

07/20/2025

My goal is to turn this bare-bones website into a personal blog! Social media has been incredibly damaging to my psyche, to say the least. All I've ever wanted was a semi-public place where I could express myself and use as a little archive to satisfy my irrational and extreme need for control and organization. And maybe also get better at talking/writing about myself instead of just holding everything in and keeping my mouth shut. I've always been good at academic writing because there's a really clear frame to follow, but when it comes to everything else, it's almost as if I'm mute. It's something I feel very insecure about, especially since my grammar just goes out the window when I start yapping like this.

It's also just hard to talk about yourself when you feel so anxious all the time, and there are a million vague, abstract, vibey thoughts and feelings floating in your brain that you have to somehow collapse into a single coherent sentence.

"How are you?? How have you been??"

I don't know!!! I feel a million different things and I don't have the words to describe them.

Anyway!!

I don't really know web design, but I do know how to code, so I'm hoping to pick it up pretty quickly along the way.
