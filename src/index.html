Home

Welcome to my home on the web!

I built this website to document everything I love. I've come to realize, in these past few years, how detrimental social media is to my mental health - and how making my own website, having control over every tiny detail of how it's presented (and how I'm represented by extension), is actually a perfect solution to all of my problems, including my irrational need for documentation and organization across many parts of my life.

I made this site for me, and me only! But I do hope you have a good time here. Please feel free to reach out and write in my guestbook!
