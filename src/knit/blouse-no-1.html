This is my first attempt at knitting a sweater. I am learning a lot about knitting in the round, shaping, and top-down construction along the way!

November 28, 2025
Didn't bother gauge swatching... knitting an XS
First time doing increases and short rows! And knitting in the round (using a mix of magic and traveling loop)
I'm using Knit Picks Palette held double - I believe this turns the yarn into worsted weight, based on the article from susannawinter.net
Frogged/restarted this 5 times :')

November 30, 2025
Finished neckline and shoulders! Watched Clo Knit's tutorial and elephaby's knit with me video for troubleshooting

December 3, 2025
I messed up the increases around the top part of one of the sleeves :/ I don't think it's noticeable so I'll keep going. I feel like with wear the fabric is going to felt anyway, so it’s fine.
There’s also a small hole at the center of the collar - it’s virtually unnoticeable so I’ll ignore it. As long as the sweater fits me right idc

December 14, 2025
Sleeves and body are now split!
The left sleeve has 1 extra stitch :/ I might do a decrease there or an increase on the right sleeve. Idk.
I'm planning to add waist shaping soon
Overall not bad for my first sweater/third knitting project!! I’m so excited to finish the body

December 19, 2025
Waist shaping plan (stolen from this article, also cross referencing Simply in Stitches' tutorials):
Work to 3 stitches before an underarm marker, k2tog, knit 1, slip marker, knit 1, ssk, repeat for the other marker
Do 1 decrease row, then 4 stockinette rows, repeat until I reach the waist then do increases (I’ll figure this out later!!)
I don’t know what I’m doing but it seems to be working so yay
I really like this yarn. it's a bit splitty but the price is low so I can't complain

December 20, 2025
I just ordered the last 2 available skeins of Lipstick from the KnitPicks website. I'm hoping that's enough to cover the sleeves, but I'm not sure.

December 22, 2025
Decreases done!!!! Took 49 rounds from the bust apex
Found another great tutorial for waist shaping! Going to follow her hip shaping/increase plan:
Side Hip Shaping Round (even underarm sts): [Work in pat to 1 st before marker, M1R, k1, sm, k1, M1L] twice—4 sts increased.
