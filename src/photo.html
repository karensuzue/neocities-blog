Photo Albums

A page for me to share cool photos of myself, friends, and family.

I try to be very selective about what I share here. I've made a promise to myself not to share any selfies unless it's a really good one. I take a lot of photos compulsively, and honestly, it's become a bit of a problem. I can easily spend hours taking pictures of myself and the things I own, then spend another few hours sorting through them, and the rest of the day ruminating over it. It's exhausting and, in my opinion, obviously unhealthy. It doesn't help that I'm recovering from over a decade of body dysmorphia.
